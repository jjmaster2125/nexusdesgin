Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web design project take?
The timeline depends on the project's complexity. A simple business website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce platforms or custom web applications may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer transparent pricing with three main options: fixed-price packages for standard websites, custom quotes for unique projects, and monthly retainers for ongoing maintenance and support. All projects include responsive design, SEO basics, and post-launch support.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include security updates, regular backups, performance optimization, and content updates. Our support plans ensure your website remains secure, fast, and up-to-date with the latest web standards.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive. They will look and function perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We test across multiple devices and browsers to ensure optimal performance.
Can you help with SEO?
Yes, we implement SEO best practices in all our websites, including proper HTML structure, fast loading times, mobile optimization, and meta tags. We also offer dedicated SEO services to improve your search rankings and drive organic traffic.
